Our Story.

Crafting luxury fragrances since 2021.

Our Journey Through Scent

2021: Brand Founded

Nawab's Perfumes was born from a passion for exquisite aromas and a vision to blend traditional craftsmanship with modern elegance.

2022: First Best-Seller Launched

"Rose Vintage" quickly became a customer favorite, establishing our reputation for quality and sophistication across the region.

2023: Expanded to International Markets

Our unique fragrances began reaching connoisseurs globally, spreading the Nawab's Perfumes experience far and wide, affirming our international appeal.

2024: Awarded "Best Niche Perfume"

Recognized by industry experts for our innovative blends and unwavering commitment to exceptional quality, a true testament to our dedication.

Founded by master perfumer Ali, Nawab's Perfumes embarked on a mission to redefine luxury fragrances. Amina's deep understanding of raw materials and her artistic flair led to the creation of scents that are not just perfumes, but experiences. From the carefully sourced rare oud to the delicate extraction of floral essences, every step in our process is a testament to our unwavering commitment to quality and artistry. We believe that a fragrance should be an extension of one's personality, a silent statement of elegance and individuality.

Our dedication extends beyond the bottle. We are committed to ethical sourcing, sustainable practices, and creating products that are both kind to the planet and luxurious for our customers. Join us on this aromatic journey and discover the essence of timeless beauty, handcrafted with passion.

Ali

Founder & Master Perfumer

"Fragrance is the art of memories, bottled."

Ali, with her profound expertise in olfactory arts, established Nawab's Perfumes with the vision of creating scents that resonate deeply. Her passion for blending rare notes and creating unique compositions is at the heart of every Nawab's Perfumes creation, ensuring each bottle is a masterpiece.

Our Core Values

Sustainability

Committed to cruelty-free practices, eco-friendly packaging, and responsible sourcing of all ingredients, minimizing our environmental footprint.

Quality

Utilizing only premium essential oils and crafting in small batches to ensure unparalleled excellence in every bottle, guaranteeing a luxurious experience.

Innovation

Continuously exploring new scent profiles and modern takes on classic fragrances, pushing the boundaries of perfumery to offer unique olfactory journeys.
