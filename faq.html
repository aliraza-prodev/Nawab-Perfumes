Frequently Asked Questions

To place a Cash on Delivery (COD) order, simply add your desired perfumes to the cart. Proceed to checkout, fill in your shipping details, and select "Cash on Delivery" as your payment method. You will pay the total amount in cash to the delivery agent when your package arrives. An SMS/email confirmation will be sent after your order is placed.

We accept returns of unopened and unused items within 7 days of delivery. The item must be in its original packaging with all seals intact. Please contact our customer support team to initiate a return. Used or opened items cannot be returned due to hygiene reasons.

Orders are typically processed and shipped within 1-2 business days. Delivery times vary based on your location:
  • Major Cities (Karachi, Lahore, Islamabad): 2-4 business days
  • Other Cities: 3-7 business days
You will receive an SMS/email with tracking details once your order has been dispatched.

If you need to change your shipping address, please contact our customer support immediately at support@noirandor.pk or +92 300 1234567. We can only accommodate changes if the order has not yet been dispatched.

Once your order is shipped, you will receive an SMS/email with a tracking number and a link to the courier's tracking page. You can use this number to monitor the status of your delivery.
